What Is Dior Known For

His fashion house is known all around the world, having gained prominence "on five continents in only a decade." Dior's skills led to his employment and design for various fashion icons in attempts to preserve the fashion industry during World War II.

What Is Unique About Dior

SAVOIR-FAIRE. Christian Dior's most precious heritage, savoir-faire inspired by excellence, is celebrated in every creation crafted by the House, from haute couture to ready-to-wear, from jewelry to home accessories.
