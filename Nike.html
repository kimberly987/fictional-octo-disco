Summary of Nike

Nike, Inc., is one of the largest and best-recognized global sports and athleticwear brands. Its extensive lineup includes its long-running Air Jordan, Air Force 1, and other “Air” models.

What is Nike Known For

Nike is best known for being a global leader in the design, manufacturing, and marketing of athletic footwear, apparel, and accessories. The company is widely recognized for its innovative products, iconic branding, and association with sports and fitness
